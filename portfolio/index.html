About Me

Hi world! My name is Chelsea Wyatt. I am from Central New Jersey, but currently reside in Pittsburgh. I am studying Communications and Public and Professional Writing at the University of Pittsburgh. With these majors in mind, I wish to enter the fields of Public Relations and Advertising. Outside of class, I enjoy exploring the city of Pittsburgh through food and outings with friends. In my free time you can find me creating playlists, watching music videos, and hosting listening parties.
